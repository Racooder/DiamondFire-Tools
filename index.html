Diamondfire Text

A tool for converting normal text into text that is optimized for diamondfire

Prettier Characters

Your text is converted into characters that are prettier than minecraft's standard font. Just enter your text in the yellow box and the prettier version is generated instantly!

HTML Color Codes

Enter a three or six characters long html color code lead by a hashtag, to translate it into a form that is understandable for diamondfire!

Back to the roots

If you want a character as it is, just place a backslash in front of it and it will not be converted!
